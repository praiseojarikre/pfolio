About Me

Frontend Developer!

As a React frontend developer, my primary role is to create and maintain the user interface of web applications using the React library. I work with designers and back-end developers to translate user requirements into interactive and visually appealing web pages. My responsibilities include writing clean and reusable code, implementing responsive designs that work seamlessly across various devices and browsers, ensuring web accessibility, and optimizing web page performance. To accomplish these tasks, I make good use of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as other tools and libraries such as React,Node.js. I also collaborate with the back-end team to integrate front-end features with the server-side logic. Overall, as a React frontend developer, i play a crucial role in delivering a quality user experience and ensuring the success of web applications.

Mechanical Engineer!

As a recent mechanical engineering graduate, my role typically involves designing, developing, and testing mechanical components and systems. I work closely with senior engineers and project managers to create engineering designs, models, and prototypes using computer-aided design (CAD) software and other tools. My day-to-day responsibilities includes analyzing project requirements, performing calculations, and simulations to evaluate designs for performance, reliability, and safety. I also create technical drawings, specifications, and reports to communicate my ideas and results to colleagues and clients. In addition to design work, i assist in the development of manufacturing processes and production plans, troubleshoot technical issues, and collaborate with other engineering disciplines to ensure the successful integration of mechanical systems. My role as a recent mechanical engineer graduate will require me to stay up-to-date with the latest technologies, trends, and best practices in mechanical engineering. I also need to have excellent problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and a strong ability to work in a team environment.

Teacher!

As a teacher/tutor, my duty is to educate and guide students in their learning journey. I work with individuals or groups of students to help them achieve their academic goals and develop their knowledge and skills in a particular subject or subjects. My responsibilities include planning and delivering lessons, designing curriculum materials, and assessing student progress through various means such as quizzes, exams, and assignments. I also provide individualized instruction, support, and feedback to students to help them improve their academic performance. In addition to teaching and assessing, i also collaborate with other educators, parents, and stakeholders to create a positive and effective learning environment. Overall, my role as a teacher/tutor is crucial in shaping the intellectual and personal development of my students. I have the opportunity to inspire and motivate students to become lifelong learners and achieve their full potential.
